How account deletion works

Short guide for users and Play Store reviewers:

1
Request deletion
Request from the app or enter your email above. We will send a confirmation link to the account's email.
2
Confirm
The link expires in 24 hours. Clicking it confirms you want to permanently delete the account.
3
Deleted
On confirmation we remove the account and associated data from our servers.
4
App cleanup
The page redirects to triplanai://app/login. The app listens for this deep link, clears local caches and shows the login screen.
