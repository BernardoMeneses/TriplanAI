About this deletion (for users and Play Store reviewers)

How it works:

  • 1) You request account deletion from the app or by entering your email above.
  • 2) We send a confirmation link to the account email. The link expires in 24 hours.
  • 3) Click the link to confirm. The confirmation page will permanently remove your account and data.
  • 4) After deletion the page will redirect to triplanai://app/login. The app will clear local cached data and show the login screen.
